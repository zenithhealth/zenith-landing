I have hard news to share: Zenith Health is shutting down, and June 10th will be the last day to access Penny and the Pregnancy Evidence Project.

A few things I want you to know:

  • Your data is yours. We will never share, sell, or compromise your information in any way as part of this shut-down process. When the platform closes, all data and user information will be securely deleted.

  • If you have any questions or concerns, you can reach out via the support tab directly in the app, or send us an email at support@zenithhealth.io. We will respond quickly to ensure the transition is as smooth as possible for you.

We set out to bring clarity, context, and confidence to a part of the women’s health journey that is so often filled with overwhelm, confusion, and anxiety. For every question you trusted Penny with, and every experience shared to the Evidence Project — thank you for trusting us. While this is not a message we ever hoped to write, it has been a true privilege to support you through your pregnancy journey.

With care and with evidence always,
